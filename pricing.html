Pricing FAQ

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, EFT payments, and cash. Payment plans are available for learnership programs.

Are there any additional fees?

All prices include training materials and certification. The only additional fees would be for on-site training or specialized equipment requirements.

Do you offer discounts for groups?

Yes, we offer significant discounts for employers and groups. The quote calculator automatically applies these discounts when you select the employer option.

Is VAT included in the prices?

All prices shown exclude VAT. VAT will be added at the current rate of 15% during the checkout process.
